HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Score more points than your opponent across 8 drives.

CARDS: Each card is either OFFENSE (orange) or DEFENSE (blue).

3 LANES: Each lane has TWO field-position bars — your drive (bottom) and theirs (top). 🏈 markers show current field position.

STARTING POSITION: Each drive starts at a random kickoff return spot (15-35 yard line).

THE PUSH: Your OFFENSE in a lane moves YOUR ball forward. Their DEFENSE pushes it back. (Net = your OFF − their DEF, ~10 yds per 25 power.)

🏈 TOUCHDOWN (6 pts): Reach 100 yards on your drive.

+1 PAT: Auto if you have a Kicker (K) in that lane → 7 pts.

+2 CONVERSION: More OFF cards than they have DEF → roll 7+ on 2d6 → 8 pts.

🛡 SAFETY (2 pts): Push their ball past 0.

🎯 PICK-SIX (6 pts): Safety + 4+ DBs (CB/S) in lane.

🟡 LANE MODIFIERS: Every match each lane has a random condition (weather, tactics, position bonuses, or wild rules). Tap one to read it.

🌟 SYNERGIES: Stack the right combos in a lane to trigger bonuses:

🤝 PLAY ACTION — QB + RB → +12 OFF
📡 SPREAD — 3+ WR → +6 OFF each
🛡️ POCKET — QB + 2+ OL → QB +15 OFF
🎯 RED ZONE — TE + QB → +8 OFF each
🏃 WILDCAT — 2+ RB → +10 OFF each
🎩 TRICK PLAY — WR + RB + TE → +5 OFF each
🎖️ FIELD GEN — 5-cost QB + 2 receivers → +15 OFF
🦅 ZONE D — 2+ CB → +5 DEF each
💪 STRONG SAFETY — S + LB → +6 DEF each
⛓️ STACKED FRONT — 3+ DL → +8 DEF each
🎯 BLITZ — LB + DE → +7 DEF each
🤝 SECONDARY — 2 CB + S → +5 DEF each
🏈 KICKING UNIT — K + ST → FG range 35+ instead of 50+

AFTER SCORING: Lane clears, fresh kickoff.

ENERGY: Drive N grants +N energy. Unspent carries over (cap 10).

SLOTS: Up to 8 cards per side per lane.
